Refund Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. General Policy

All sales are generally final once access is delivered. Please review plan details carefully before purchase.

2. Possible Refund Exceptions

  • Duplicate charge for the same order.
  • Billing error caused by our system.
  • Non-delivery cases where support cannot provide access after reasonable troubleshooting.

3. Not Eligible for Refund

  • Change of mind after successful delivery.
  • Violations of Terms of Service, abuse, fraud, or chargeback misuse.
  • Compatibility issues caused by unsupported hardware/software not listed as supported.
  • Account or platform actions taken by third parties.

4. Subscription Cancellation

For recurring plans, cancel before your next renewal date in the Stripe Billing Portal. Cancellation stops future billing and does not retroactively refund completed billing periods unless required by law.

5. Refund Request Window

Refund requests must be submitted within 72 hours of the charge unless a longer period is required by law in your jurisdiction.

6. Chargebacks

Before filing a chargeback, contact support first. Fraudulent or abusive chargebacks may result in immediate license revocation and account termination.
