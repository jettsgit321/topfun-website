Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and checkout data: username, email, selected plan, order/session IDs.
  • Payment metadata from Stripe such as customer ID and subscription ID.
  • License and security data: license key, HWID bindings, loader token records, and anti-abuse logs.
  • Technical logs: IP address, timestamps, and request metadata for fraud prevention and service stability.

2. How We Use Data

  • To create and deliver purchased access.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To enforce device-slot limits, detect abuse, and secure accounts.
  • To provide support and communicate transactional updates.

3. Service Providers

We share relevant data with service providers that power the platform, including Stripe (payments), Resend (delivery email), and KeyAuth (license provisioning and revocation).

4. Retention

We retain order, billing, and security records as long as reasonably necessary for service operation, legal compliance, dispute handling, and fraud prevention.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or information about data use. California residents may have rights under the CCPA/CPRA.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use essential session and security mechanisms needed to run checkout, delivery, anti-abuse limits, and account continuity.

7. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no system can be guaranteed completely secure.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy by posting a revised version on this page.
